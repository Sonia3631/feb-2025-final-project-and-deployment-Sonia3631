Our Story

Discover the philosophy behind our sanctuary of wellness.

Creating a Haven of Peace

Founded in 2010, Serenity Retreat began as a vision to create an urban oasis where people could escape the stresses of modern life. Our founder, Olivia Bennett, combined her expertise in holistic therapies with luxurious spa treatments to create a unique wellness experience.

Today, our award-winning spa continues this tradition, offering innovative treatments while maintaining our commitment to personalized care and exceptional service.
